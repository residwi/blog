Hi, I'm Resi. I'm a software engineer, mostly backend, based in Indonesia, with 5+ years of experience. I like building APIs, working with databases, CI/CD, and infrastructure (Docker, Kubernetes, GCP), and figuring out how things work under the hood. I'm endlessly curious and always learning something new.

## Where I've worked

- **Vidio** (Aug 2021 to present), Software Engineer. Ruby on Rails and Go. I worked on the ads team, then moved to the subscriptions and payments team. We practice pair programming and TDD.
- **DQLab** (Jan 2021 to Aug 2021), Backend Engineer. Keycloak for auth, GitLab CI, Kubernetes.
- **Altomatik** (Nov 2019 to Dec 2020), Backend Engineer. Symfony and PHP, REST APIs, integrating a tour-and-travel system with a booking engine.
- **Futurax** (2018), freelance full-stack. PHP and Bootstrap.

I studied Informatics at Universitas Esa Unggul (B.Eng, 2016 to 2020) on a full scholarship.

## Why this blog exists

If I solve a problem once, I'd rather write it down than solve it again from scratch. This site is how I learn in public and keep a personal knowledge base. If it helps someone else along the way, even better.

## What you'll find here

- **Posts**: longer brain dumps and technical write-ups.
- **TILs (Today I Learned)**: short, quick things I picked up day to day.

I don't write on a schedule. I write when something is worth keeping.
